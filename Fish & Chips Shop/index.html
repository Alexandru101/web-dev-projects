About Us

Welcome to Dr Vinegars!
Located in the heart of the community, Dr Vinegars Extraordinary Fish & Chips is dedicated to serving the perfect British staple. We believe that great fish and chips start with the basics: fresh, sustainably sourced fish and the finest local potatoes, prepared daily on-site.
Our secret? It’s all in the "extraordinary" touch. Whether it's our light, golden crispy batter or our signature salt and vinegar finish, we take pride in every portion we wrap. From our jumbo sausages to our fresh-carved doner meat, we’re more than just a chippy—we’re a local tradition.
